Divine in the Matrix

In this series of digital artworks Nomie wanted to invite the viewer to contemplate the interplay between creation and destruction. This piece was drawn in Procreate and glitched using Text Edit and Audacity by breaking the code of the images. She wanted to convey the idea that there is beauty in destruction. The glitched images, with their distorted lines and vibrant colors, convey a sense of chaos and entropy. However, as the viewer looks closer, they will begin to see the divine patterns that emerge from the chaos. Destruction and creation are intertwined and that beauty can be found even in the midst of chaos.

Metamorphosis or Bloom

"Metamorphosis or Bloom” is a surreal, collage art that depicts both spiritual and physical transformation at a personal level. Butterflies and fungi are often viewed as metaphors for rebirth and resurrection, for the triumph of the spirit and the soul over the physical prison.
